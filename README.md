# WHO WE ARE

**V0IDNETWORK** is an independent engineering and research collective founded by **Iliya** and **Ehsan**.

We study how modern Internet infrastructure actually works—not only in theory, but under real-world conditions.

Our work combines software engineering, networking, distributed systems and protocol research to build resilient infrastructure and document the Internet from the protocol level upward.

> **Build systems. Break assumptions. Understand protocols. Share knowledge.**
