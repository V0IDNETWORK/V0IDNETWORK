## Professional Summary

**V0IDNETWORK** is a specialized network security research initiative focused on **protocol-level analysis of censorship and surveillance infrastructure**. The work spans deep packet inspection (DPI) systems, circumvention protocol engineering, reverse engineering of filtering mechanisms, and development of open-source tools for network transparency.

Core competency: taking undocumented, proprietary, or poorly understood network behaviors and producing rigorous, reproducible technical documentation.

## Selected Research & Projects

**Deep Packet Inspection Research**
Protocol-level dissection of commercial and state-deployed DPI systems. Documents classification heuristics, fingerprinting methods, and evasion vectors.

**Circumvention Protocol Engineering**
Design and implementation of tools that evade traffic classification at the application and transport layers. Research-first approach with full technical write-ups.

**Surveillance Infrastructure Analysis**
Documentation of passive and active monitoring deployments. Focus on reproducible methodology and public disclosure.

**Security Awareness Tooling**
Educational demonstrations (e.g., Telegram social engineering demo) designed to inform defenders rather than enable attacks. Full disclosure policy.

## The Problem We're Solving

Billions of people live behind Internet censorship systems. Those systems are sophisticated, adaptive, and largely undocumented in technical detail. The result: circumvention tools are built on incomplete understanding, break under pressure, and fail the people who need them most.

**V0IDNETWORK exists to change that.**

We produce the deep technical documentation that the circumvention ecosystem is missing — so developers can build tools that actually hold up against state-level adversaries.
