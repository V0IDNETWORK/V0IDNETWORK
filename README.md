## 🌌 About V0IDNETWORK

**V0IDNETWORK** is an ongoing, open research effort to document — rigorously and accurately — how the modern Internet's **circumvention** and **surveillance** technologies actually work at the **protocol level**, in support of a more open and resilient Internet.

We dive deep into the wires, packets, and shadows of the network. From DPI systems and filtering mechanisms to advanced censorship circumvention techniques — everything is analyzed, documented, and shared openly.

- **🛡️ Mission**: Transparency in the age of surveillance.
- **🔬 Focus**: Protocol-level understanding of the Internet.
- **🌍 Goal**: Empower users, researchers, and developers to build stronger, freer networks.
